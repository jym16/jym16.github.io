My work with Prof. Kousuke Yakubo (Hokkaido Univ.) and Gentaro Shimojo (Hokkaido Univ.) will be presented at The Physical Society of Japan 2023 Autumn Meeting which takes place at Tohoku University in Sendai, Japan on 16th September, 2023.
Unfortunately, I will not be able to attend in person but Gentaro will be there to present our work which discusses walk dimensions and spectral dimensions in fractal scale-free networks.
